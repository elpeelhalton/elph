About Student Resources

Experiential learning opportunities allow students to explore their strengths and interests, while developing essential skills for the workforce. It aims for you to understand what you have learnt in the classroom, and apply that in a practical setting in a field of your choice. Some experiential learning opportunities include co-ops, internships, and apprenticeships. The following guide helps you to further understand what experiential learning is, how to get the best opportunity, as well as resume, and interview walkthroughs.
